About

co-founder and CEO of snap Inc

Evan Thomas Spiegel is an American businessman, co-founder and CEO of Snap Inc. Spiegel was the youngest billionaire in the world in 2015. As of March 2023, he had a personal net worth of $2.7 billion according to Forbes.
Born: 4 June 1990 (age 33 years), Los Angeles, California, United States
Net worth: 310 crores USD (2023) Forbes
Spouse: Miranda Kerr (m. 2017)
Parents: John W. Spiegel, Melissa Ann Thomas
Siblings: Caroline Spiegel, Lauren Spiegel
Nationality: American, French

Versailles Award

After David Rockefeller Jr., it is the founder of Snapchat who will be honored with this award, which was created in 2019 to celebrate American philanthropy. The award is in recognition of Evan Spiegel's personal donation - naturalized a French citizen last year - in support of the Palace of Versailles.
